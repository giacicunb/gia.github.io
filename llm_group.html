Study Group on Large Language Models

Welcome to this space dedicated to learning and deepening knowledge in LLM. In this group, we will have the opportunity to share knowledge, debate ideas, and enhance our skills in LLM together.

We give preference to students interested in working on their thesis with a theme related to LLMs.

There are various LLMs in the market! I can mention a few: GPT, Llama, Bloom, Falcon, Mistral. Do you have computational resources ($$$) to run some of these?

Group Objectives:
  • Organize students interested in studying LLM and arrange computational resources for experiments.
  • Weekly meetings for lectures, discussions, and problem-solving.
  • Problem-solving-based learning methodology.
  • Raise funds to execute LLMs.
  • Request credits for cloud computing (Google Cloud research credits).
