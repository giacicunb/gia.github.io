The Artificial Intelligence Group from University of Brasilia (Grupo de Inteligência Artificial - GIA) develops research, teaching and extension activies related to Artificial Intelligence (AI). The main GIA research works are related to Machine Learning, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision and Bioinspired Computing. Additionally, Databases and BigData, Data Visualization and Visual Analytics are employed to support tasks related to the main research areas aforementioned.

Nowadays, GIA group maintain research cooperations with Brazilian and abroad groups. In Brazil, our reseachers have been working with other researchers from University of São Paulo (USP), São Paulo State University (Unesp), Aeronautics Institute of Technology (Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica - ITA) and Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG). Moreover, GIA researchers are currently collaborating with research abroad groups, such as University of Tsukuba, George Mason University (GMU), University of California, Davis (UC Davis), University of New South Wales (UNSW Sydney), U.Porto, Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e) and University of Münster (Westfälische Wilhelms-Universität Münster).
