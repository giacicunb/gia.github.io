Workshop Objectives

The primary objective of this workshop is to disseminate the wide range of research, extension, and teaching initiatives related to Artificial Intelligence. It aims to showcase practical applications of AI in diverse fields. Through informative lectures, engaging case studies, and interactive, hands-on activities, participants will gain valuable insights into diverse AI research and its real-world implementations across multiple sectors.

The dissemination of research conducted at UnB to the broader community holds significant importance for multiple reasons. Primarily, it ensures the community can access new knowledge and scientific breakthroughs. Additionally, considering the escalating interest and emerging discoveries in AI, fostering engagement and encouraging community involvement becomes crucial. Such active participation can foster partnerships and collaborations between the university and community members, leading to joint projects, knowledge exchange, and remarkable advancements across various domains.

The target audience for this workshop includes undergraduate and graduate students, technology professionals, data scientists, software engineers, and individuals with a keen interest in exploring the advancements in Artificial Intelligence at UnB. It provides an excellent opportunity for all these individuals to gain insights into the ongoing AI initiatives and research at UnB.

Esteemed researchers and professors from UnB, recognized leaders in AI research, development, and teaching, will conduct this workshop. They will generously share their invaluable experiences, profound insights, and groundbreaking advancements with the diverse UnB community. Participants can expect to benefit from the expertise and knowledge of these esteemed professionals during the workshop.
