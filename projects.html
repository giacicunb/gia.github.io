Past Projects

KnEDLe

The goal of this project was to develop intelligent tools for extracting structured information from publications of the Diário Oficial do Distrito Federal. For that purpose, As a result, the search and information retrieval, increasing government transparency and facilitating audit tasks and detecting problems related to the use of public resources.

Principal Investigator: Prof. Dr. Thiago de Paulo Faleiros
Sponsor: Fundação de Apoio à Pesquisa do Distrito Federal (FAPDF) Period: 2020-2023

HCIA-AI

This project provides the essential training for professionals who wish to work in the field of Technology and also prepares the student for the Huawei Certified ICT Associate (HCIA) certification. Upon completion of the course, the student can take practice exams through Huawei's TALENT ONLINE platform and request a free voucher to take the certification exam.

Principal Investigator: Prof. Dr. Priscila America Solis Mendez
Sponsor: Fundação de Apoio À Pesquisa (FUNAPE) Period: 2022-2022

Brasilia Living Labs

This document presents a research project for the implementation of a center for identification and demonstration of technologies applied to enhance efficiency in urban dynamics and evidence-based simulation - the Urban Efficiency Center, Brasília Living Labs. The project is developed in response to the demands of the Ministry of Science, Technology, Innovation, and Communications (MCTIC) and the Government of the Federal District.

Principal Investigator: Prof. Dr. Jorge Henrique Cabral Fernandes
Sponsor: Ministry of Science, Technology, Innovation, and Communications (MCTIC) Period: 2020-2020
